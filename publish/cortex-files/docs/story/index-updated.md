## üé≠ The Story

In a dingy basement laboratory somewhere in the wilds of New Jersey, a mad scientist named **Asifinstein** made a horrifying discovery: his brilliant AI assistant, GitHub Copilot, had **complete amnesia**. Every conversation started from zero. Every coffee break erased everything.

This is the story of how Asifinstein spent seventy-two hours (fueled by pizza rolls and Mountain Dew) building **CORTEX** - a sophisticated cognitive architecture that transforms Copilot from a forgetful intern into an expert development partner.

But the journey didn't stop there. Over the next year, CORTEX evolved from a brilliant prototype into a production-ready, self-sustaining development partner that remembers everything, learns from every feature, and can even fix itself.
